UX Design - Usability Research - Product Vision

I've worn the hats.

Developer. Researcher. Program Manager. Designer.
These are the roles that I have held since entering the industry. Each time I felt unchallenged, I expanded my role, responsibilities and capabilities, before finally settling on UX Design. These experiences have been cumulative, and I use my knowledge and skills to enhance my work and relationships with clients. I do not back down from a challenge... or the opportunity to expand my skillset.

I have worked at BlackBerry for over 5 years, and have been involved in a multitude of projects on mobile, desktop and accessories.

Heuristic evaluations, usability sessions, focus groups and quantitative resesearch.

Delivery and management of in-house and outsourced resources on over 18 projects simultaneously.

Competitive analysis, story and roadmap planning, evangelizing the value of design.

Low and high fidelity wireframes, flows, interactive prototypes, UX QA, and conceptual explorations.

StormHunters MUD


I took ownership of this MUD (text-based multiplayer game) in 2007, and began overhauling features over the course of the next few years with a team of three. Work included C/C++ development, two iterations of websites and forums (one as a 24-hour challenge), feature roadmapping, market analysis, creative writing, world creation, and quality assurance.


KEY ACCOMPLISHMENTS

  • Complete conversion of a flatfile saving system into an account-based system
  • Overhaul of skill and class system with over interconnected 200 abilities
  • Updated item customization system including pseudo-UI
  • Random quest generator to provide gameplay variety and retain player interest
  • Introduction of a gathering/crafting system inspired by other MMORPGs
