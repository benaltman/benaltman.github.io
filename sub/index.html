User-Centered Design. On Time.

Having worked as a developer, user researcher, program manager and UX designer, I've become accustomed to the needs and expectations of each role. As a result, I can speak their language when necessary, and make sure that the best result is produced. By understanding technical limitations and market needs, I reduce churn and produce a user-centered design.

Usability research, heuristic evaluation and competitive analysis provide the groundwork for user needs.

Iterative, agile design produces wireframes with a splash of colour. Easy collaboration with development and UI Design.

Management of resources and schedules to ensure things get out the door on time. Deep dives on UX quality and accuracy.
